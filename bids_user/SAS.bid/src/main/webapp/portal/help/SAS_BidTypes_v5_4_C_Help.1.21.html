Vacation bid types : New Vacation
New Vacation
Through the Vacation bid you can bid for a vacation period in the open bid window period. Max number of bids depends on season. Insert your preferred period in Alt 1. Insert an alternative period in Alt2, this is good in case your Alt 1 cannot be granted, e.g. due to lack of quota. Press OK.
In case you want to place one more Vacation bid, select Place Bid again.
Input
Alt 1 (From)
Select your preferred start date of vacation period.
Alt 1 (To)
Select your preferred end date of vacation period.
Alt 2 (From)
Select alternative start date to preferred start date.
Alt 2 (To)
Select alternative end date to preferred start date.
Etc...
As above.
Comment
Optional: Insert a comment that you want the Crew Planner to know of. Maximum 200 characters are allowed.
