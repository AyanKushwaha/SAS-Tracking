Modifying Global bids
To modify Global bids:
1.
In the Bid explorer, select Global bids. In the Bid work area, double-click the Global bid you want to modify or select the bid and click Edit> Open
The Global Bids form opens.
2.
Modify the Global bids and click OK.
The Global bid is updated and its content is updated in the Global bids overview in the Calendar.
