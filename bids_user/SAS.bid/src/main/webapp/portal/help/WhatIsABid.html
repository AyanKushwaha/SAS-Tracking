Crew Portal Bid User Guide : Bidding overview : Bids and bid points : What is a bid?
What is a bid?
A bid is your personal preference of a specific roster property. For example “I want a free weekend in the beginning of May” or “I want late check-ins every Monday”.
Bids are organized in collections of bids, otherwise known as bid scenarios. In turn, each bid scenario belongs to a bid category.
Bid scenarios
Bid scenarios are collections of bids and are used to sort and pool bids, for example for different periods or for bids with different properties. Having more than one bid scenario in the weighted bid model makes it possible for crew members to evaluate different bidding strategies using Interbid’s decision support tools, before ultimately picking the bid scenario they want Crew Portal Bid to consider as their final bids. Another use of bid scenarios is to keep good summer bids hidden from the optimizer during the winter schedule.
Bid categories
Bid categories are collections of bid scenarios. By using bid categories, multiple sets of bids are supported. Dependent on the selected bid category and the bid scenario configuration, you are allowed to modify, copy or submit bid scenarios and bids within them. For example, in the Templates bid category, you can modify a bid scenario and the bids within but you cannot mark the bids as ready. The following bid categories exist:
Current
This bid category contains all bid scenarios and bids for the upcoming bid period. Bid scenarios can be marked as either finalized or set to draft.
Standing
Bids in this category are continuously available: they are not tied to a particular period and will only be considered in the absence of any Current bids.
Templates
Bids in this category are continuously available and can be used again at some point in the future. To use bids in the Template category for the upcoming bid period, they are copied to the Current category. After that, you may need to modify date properties for the active bid period.
Global
Bids in this category are contained in one bid scenario and prominently displayed above the Bid work area, in the Calendar. Global bids are automatically applied to other bid scenarios when they are finalized. You can modify Global bids in the Bid manager.
History
This category contains all bid scenarios and bids from the current and standing categories for closed bid windows. To reuse bids in this category, they can be copied to the other bid categories.
