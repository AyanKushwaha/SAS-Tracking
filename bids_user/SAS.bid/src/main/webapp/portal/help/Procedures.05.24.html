Viewing Trip Points
To view points that the optimizer will give your trips that match bids in the selected bid scenario. The result is displayed in the Trip Points window. This view helps you to determine if your bids result in a correct ranking of matching trips before finalizing the bids.
To view Trip Points:
1.
In the Bid explorer, select a bid scenario.
2.
In the Bid explorer, select Tools> View Trip Points.
