Crew Portal Bid User Guide : Bidding overview
Bidding overview
Note
This section is applicable on Activity & Time Off bids, see Activity & Time Off orientation for more information.
Preferential bidding is based on the fact that people have different needs. The same roster may be considered good by one person and bad by another, and switching duties between two equivalent crew members may add quality of life to both. Crew Portal Bid supports both the weighted bid model and bid group model (strictly ordered bids).
