PBS Bid types : Stop
Stop
Use this bid to request pairings that have night stops at a specific destination(s). This is a general bid, which is considered granted when the length of the stop is withing the minimum and maximum hours on any day of the month.
Combine this bid with the “Nights away from home base” Lifestyle to specify your preferred layover station. This bid can also be combined with other lifestyles of your choice.
Input
Destination
Insert preferred layover station. Select one of the destinations from the drop down menu.
Minimum stop length
Insert minimum stop time
Maximum stop length
Insert maximum stop time
Priority
Select bid priority – High, Medium or Low

The priority method works as follows:

If possible, High will always be prioritized above Medium and Low. The same applies to a Medium bid over a Low bid. A High bid also has higher priority than a Medium and Low bid combined.
