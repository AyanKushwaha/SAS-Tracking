Crew Info
The Leave window contains information about the crew member logged on, as for example balance, seniority, entitlement, qualification, or contract details.
Crew information is displayed in the following locations:
•
On one or several rows between the Leave calendar and the Leave bid work area.
•
In the Crew Info view. The Crew Info view is located on the right side in the Leave window. To hide the Crew Info view in the Leave window, click . To display it again, click .
