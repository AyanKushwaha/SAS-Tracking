Activity & Time Off orientation
Crew Portal Bid contains the Activity & Time Off tab. In this tab you can perform the following bidding activities:
•
view and analyze pre-assignments
•
design your roster by submitting requests and preferences
•
create and submit new bids
•
analyze bids
•
manage existing bids.
